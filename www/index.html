About

How can you provide easy information about diet and exercise to maintain an ideal BMI? Obesity is a global issue with adverse health effects. Existing BMI calculators and fitness apps often lack accuracy and tailored recommendations. This app integrates BMI prediction, meal suggestions, and exercise recommendations. It can also support healthcare professionals and fitness experts in monitoring their clients' progress. The goals is on accurate BMI prediction and evaluating personalized recommendations' effectiveness for healthier living.
